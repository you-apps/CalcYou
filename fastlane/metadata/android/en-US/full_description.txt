CalcYou is a privacy focused calculator app that is not just a calculator.

Solve everyday problems with the intuitive basic calculator, or delve into advanced functions with the powerful scientific calculator.

Feeling lost in a sea of units? CalcYou's versatile unit converter can convert lengths, weights, volumes, and more.

Need to express complex equations? Access a vast library of symbols, including Greek letters, arrows, superscript, and subscript, all with a few taps.

You want to quickly draw a mathematical function to understand its shape? No worries, CalcYou can plot it for you.
